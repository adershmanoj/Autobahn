It was 3 years back when we started off this journey.We propelled Triva Yanthram, we imparted vigour to its powerhouse. The Motörheads of SCT redefined the complexion of an Auto-fest, a first of its kind in Kerala. They termed this venture by the name of the German Wonderland, the Autobahn, where dreams come true for any car enthusiast. Yes, so did our seniors, to enliven the vision of Autobahn 2013 and Autobahn 2014. They launched themselves ‘Gearing to Shift’ , later ‘Shifting to Overdrive’. Its time we pull the trigger for the 2017 edition with all your support and backing.

1999
HAPPENED , FOR IT WAS MEANT TO

The 90's had to end someway and AAES decieded to end it with a bang , by bringing up the first ever national level motor show organised by any students association.MOTORSHOW '99 was a grand gala event which was a veritable wedding of the beauties of the road.The show included all the then latest models of different classes of vehicles.Over 25,000 people witnessed the 72 hours of the non-stop mega event , which proved to be a culture that would go a long way.

2000
THE MILLENIUM AUTOSHOW

This time AAES associated with the Institute of Engineers , India to bring forth the spectacle ; AutoFest 2000.The millenium year dawned as a testimony that the previous year's success , wasn't any beginners luck , but conducting an extravagant show with seminars on Preventive maintainance of new gen automobiles.

2001
FOR NOTHING LESS THAN BEST

Continuing their success and raising their standards every year AAES organized the 3rd edition of their renowned auto show.The students association now made it a habit to compromise for nothing less than the best by venturing a show that included technical seminars taken by eminent personalities from the automotive industry , quiz competitions , group disscussions and interactive sessions for students as well as the public.

2002
A TRUE SUCCESS STORY

As years passed by , the public expected the very next edition of the MotorShow.AAES never forgot to provide their eager crowd here in Trivandrum.So thus the curtains were raised for MOTORSHOW '02 , with a high spirited participation from most of the prominent automobile manufacturers making AAES a topic of discussion all over the nation ; a true success story inscribed in golden letters.

2003
THE GIANTS IN THE INDUSTRY NOW
PREFERRED THE AAES PLATFORM

The fifth venture of the auto-expo MOTORSHOW -03 was inquired by industries more than it was promoted.An extravagant rally comprising of more than 150 motorbikes took the baton forward to inaugurate the show , while the automobile giants preferred to unveil their best products like Toyota Camry , Ford Mondeo , Mahindra Scorpio and many more to the public.As part of it , a spectacular cultural fest staging a vibrant fashion show and a dazzling dance fest attracted talents from all major colleges around the nation.

2004
A GROWTH SO IMMINENT

The participation of some of the major automotive industries in the prestigious autoexpo of the previous year attracted even more companies to MOTORSHOW '04.A fest needing no introduction , needed no further contemplation.The crowd and the students came together once again to take AAES to better heights by attending enthusiastically in seminars and exhibition to be part of it , rather than just staying in the galleries to see.

2005
A complete fest for a vast crowd

The fame spreaded territories faster than light to conquer hearts of many aspiring students, which was a driving force in making them travel thousands of kilometers to be a part of this spectacle earthen than just read about them in literals. MOTORSHOW '05 showed us to never set a limit to what we can achieve. The show always provided the crowd with nothing less than the best including talk shows and seminars of some of the pinnacle automotive leaders, now crossing boundaries of our nation.

2007
A name that took off
and never saw a set of tail lights
ever

The German freeway, the dream of every automobile enthusiast worth his salt, a road with no limits, it's name meant speed and the only thing that came close happened to be our fest. And so, it happened- AUTOBAHN '07. A fest revamped, entirely in it's aesthetics and functionality. Doubts flew off the walls when the sincere fan base, of AAES's fame and pride, whole heartedly accepted the latest edition of the fest that saw the most modern methodologies in conducting an outstanding expo, paper presentations and seminars. The fest also staged a full fledged cultural fest with a crowd turn-up seen never before, hinting that the best days are yet to come.

2013
The Milestone event

The success in the year 2007, only inspired the students and AAES to set new records and climb higher to the peak of satisfaction. All the inspiration and history drove the minds to bring together one if the best festivals ever organized by a student body all over the nation. AUTOBAHN '13, staged the best from the automotive universe with programs like auto-expo, obstacle race, dirt racing, auto-modding competitions, Gokarting, technical expo, parking challenge and a never seen before death defying stunt show by GhostRyderZ. A perfect matchup that had no equal for anytime to come.

2014
Bigger got better

The previous year's Autobahn was now the benchmark for colleges around the nation, making every automobile enthusiast to better his skills for the better repeatedly, since the expertise of the self-proclaimed fest made it too good to catch-up. Thus bigger, got better, with AUTOBAHN '14, which yet again spread waves around the public and changed notions to nothing close to a mediocre expo. The year saw a fest most widely publicized with programs like auto-expo, Apex racing workshops, Gokarting, auto-quiz, pitstop and out of the blue, a show by the true champion, the Polish Prodigy, Rafal Pasierbek a.k.a Stunter13. Thus only AUTOBAHN could leave a crowd both speechless and make them scream with awe.

2017
AutoBahn '17

Now, it's the start of a new year. An year promised to witness the greatest and most anticipated auto-fest of our people. This time, it's not about technical prowess, it's not about fame, it's simply about reaching to the depths of every Motörhead's soul and sending a message. A message that, it ain't the white collars that keeps our head high, but our greasy hands.
We are a team of individuals or rather a family of high spirited pioneers in the making, who wouldn't stand for anything other than total victory and anything less than that is failure. Burnt rubber, chequered flags, flaming turbos..you're in the land of the Relentless. This is AUTOBAHN.

The highlight of autobahn has always been it's autoexpo.Infact the first ever auto expo in the state of Kerala was organised by the AAES of SCTCE under the name Motorshow which was later re-christened to AUTOBAHN.From luxury vehicles worth crores to out of the world modifications,Autobahn has featured it all.Autobahn has pioneeredd perfection when it comes to its autoexpo and has grabbed headline time and again for being the best autoexpo in the state.Autobahn has drawn attention for its sprawling dome,flawless arrangements,automobiles on display and huge crowd attending the expo.The execution of the expo has been exponentially well managed in the past with a record of 100% satisfaction on part of many participants and viewers,regarding the condition of the vehicles.In fact Tata Motors chose autobahn to launch the fanes Tata Indica for the first time in Kerala.The success of that launch is evident from the fact that Tata motors chose the same venue a decade later to launch the last version of Indica, the Indica D90.
